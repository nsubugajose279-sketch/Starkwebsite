The BMW M8 Competition is a high-performance luxury vehicle offered as a coupe, convertible, or Gran Coupe (a four-door sedan). It is powered by a 617-horsepower twin-turbo 4.4-liter V8 engine, capable of accelerating from 0 to 60 mph in about 3 seconds. Key features include a rear-biased all-wheel-drive system, an 8-speed automatic transmission, M sport suspension and brakes, and luxurious M-specific interior and exterior styling.

Engine:A 4.4-liter, twin-turbo V8 producing 617 horsepower and 553 lb-ft of torque. Transmission:An 8-speed M Sport automatic transmission with M Drivelogic for adjustable shift aggression. Drivetrain:A rear-biased all-wheel-drive (M xDrive) system that can send up to 100% of power to the rear wheels. Brakes:Available with high-performance carbon ceramic brakes for superior stopping power.

About Us

Uganda's Premier Sports Car Community

Stark Car Meets is Uganda's fastest-growing automotive community, dedicated to bringing together sports car enthusiasts, drifters, and rally fans. Based in Uganda , we organize regular events that showcase the best of Uganda's automotive culture.

Our mission is to create a safe, inclusive environment where car lovers can connect, share knowledge, and experience the thrill of high-performance vehicles. Whether you're a seasoned driver or just passionate about sports cars, there's a place for you in our community.

From organized track days and drifting competitions to casual meetups and rally events, Stark Car Meets offers diverse experiences for all automotive enthusiasts.
